About PhishGuard


PhishGuard is an interactive phishing awareness and detection platform designed to help users recognize and respond to phishing attacks effectively.


Our system combines real-world phishing scenarios, user interaction, and performance analytics to improve cybersecurity awareness across organizations.


By analyzing user decisions and detection accuracy, PhishGuard provides actionable insights to strengthen human defenses against social engineering attacks.
